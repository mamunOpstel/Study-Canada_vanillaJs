FAQs about using Simplii Financial

What is Simplii Financial?

Simplii Financial™, a division of CIBC, is a digital banking brand offering great rates and free daily banking. Bank securely whenever and wherever you like with 24/7 access to online, mobile and telephone banking as well as access to one of the largest national ATM networks in Canada through CIBC. Simplified banking is our promise to you! With Simplii Financial, you get chequing, savings, mortgage, borrowing, investment and credit cards all in one simple place.

Why do I need to set up an account prior to arriving in Canada?

Setting up and pre-funding your Canadian account prior to arrival will give you faster access to your funds immediately after arriving in Canada. It also helps to avoid the challenges associated with completing foreign exchange transfers or travelling with large amounts of cash. Additionally, you can receive your GIC certificate which can be used to support your study permit application.

What’s the processing fee to open an account via a Student GIC or Student Deposit Program?

For all accounts funded using foreign currency, Simplii Financial charges no processing fees while signing up for ISBO products via Student GIC or Student Deposits program.

For students interested in funding their account using Canadian Dollars, a processing fee of 2% is applied.

How long does it take to apply for GIC or open an account with Simplii?

The application takes around 5 minutes. In order to complete the application, you’ll need your passport and a selfie photo. Simplii usually processes your application on the same day.
