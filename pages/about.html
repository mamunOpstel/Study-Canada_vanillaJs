About Study in Canada

Study-Canada started its journey in 2008 with former Bangladeshi students who
studied abroad and intended to make the path of others easier for international
education and career development.

Services We Provide

We provide a range of support and services for any Bangladeshi
student to fulfill his/her desire of studying in Canada.

Student Profile Analysis By Study Canada

At Study-Canada, we don't just offer counseling for admission or university or college selection.
We also analyze a suitable student profileto give each student the most important choice.
